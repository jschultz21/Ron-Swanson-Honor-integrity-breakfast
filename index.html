Back to Basics: The Swanson Pyramid of Greatness

Open Forum

Public servant Ron Swanson feels that much of society has lost sight of what it takes to be an excellent human being. He has been developing his Pyramid of Greatness for years and believes it to be a perfectly calibrated recipe for maximum personal achievement. Join Ron in the SIS Founders Lounge for an open discussion on some of the important components of greatness such as capitalism, hunting, breakfast, and old ships.

Who is Ron Swanson?

Ron Swanson is the director of the parks and recreation department in Pawnee, Indiana. On the side he is a smooth jazz saxophonist and goes by Duke Silver. As a staunch libertarian, Ron Swanson believes that the phrase government efficiency is an oxymoron. Ron is also an avid hunter and fisherman, has a self described strong tolerance for alcoholic beverages and is a connoisseur of well-prepared steaks. Ron is a breakfast enthusiast, gun owner and believes that facial hair should be full thick and square - never sculpted.
